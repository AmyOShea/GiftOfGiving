How It Works

Register

You can register for the website using the sign up form. Once registered you can update your profile to either a charity or donor type when you log in. Charities will be required to send identification before being able to access the sites functionality. Information on this will be communicated on the profile page.

Choose

Verfied charities will add gift requests which can be viewed on the gifts page. A list of gifts that have not yet been recieved by the charity will be listed, some of them may of already been committed to and will not have a buy option. If you would like to purchase it, click the buy option and it will take you to the gifts page to confirm.

Send

Once you have committed to buy a gift, you will be given postal details on where to send the gift and the status of the gift will become "committed" until it's received and no buy option will be available. Once the charity have received the gift, they will update the gifts status and it will no longer display on the gifts page.
